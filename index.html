Why Dalas?

You deserve lunch you don't have to feel bad about

You're busy. You want something quick. But "quick" on the Gold Coast usually means heavy, processed, and forgettable. You eat it, you crash — and spend the rest of the afternoon wishing you'd chosen better.

Jess and Michael Davies built Dalas Kitchen because they were sick of that same old compromise. They wanted to prove that eating fast doesn't have to mean eating badly.

Every salad, sanga, and homemade soda is made fresh each day from local hinterland produce. No shortcuts. No sad iceberg lettuce. Just real food that loves you back.

The Gold Coast's only gourmet salad bar — serving Nobby Beach, Palm Beach, and soon Currumbin. Grab and go, or stay a while. Either way, you'll feel the difference.
